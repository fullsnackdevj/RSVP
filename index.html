Frequently Asked Questions

What time should we arrive?

The ceremony will begin promptly at 10:00 AM. We kindly request that guests arrive at least 30 minutes early to allow time for seating and to ensure the celebration starts smoothly.

Where is the ceremony and reception taking place?

Both will be held at White Plate Manila. The ceremony will take place indoors, followed by a lunch reception in the same area — no need to travel between venues.

What is the overall theme of the wedding?

We’ll be celebrating in a whimsical style — playful, dreamy, and full of vibrant or multicolored schemes. We kindly ask guests to observe the dress code detailed elsewhere on the site to help make the day even more special.

Can I bring a +1?

We truly appreciate your enthusiasm to celebrate with us. However, due to limited seating for this intimate wedding and our guest list, we are unable to accommodate additional guests (+1s). Thank you for your understanding.

Are children allowed?

We love your little ones — but to ensure everyone’s comfort and for planning purposes, only children who are specifically listed on the invitation may attend. Thank you for your understanding.

When is the RSVP deadline?

Kindly submit your RSVP by November 30, 2025 so we can finalize seating and other arrangements.

Is parking available?

Yes — parking is available on-site, but spaces are very limited and are available on a first-come, first-served basis. We recommend car-pooling or using ride services where possible.
